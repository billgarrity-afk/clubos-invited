Built on What You Already Have

Spectrio moves content to your screens.
ClubOS makes your screens respond.

Your clubs already run Spectrio. Your content team already has a workflow. ClubOS doesn't change any of that — it loads through the Web Page App slot already installed across your fleet and adds the one thing Spectrio can't: the ability to know who's in the room and give them something that drives them to act.
